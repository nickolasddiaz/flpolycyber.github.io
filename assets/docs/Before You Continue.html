How to Join

First read and adhere to the Cybersecurity Club Code of Conduct document.

Second go to Involve🔗 and click "Join".

Third go to our Discord Discord🔗 and click "Accept Invite". Once in the Discord, the "EmailBot" from "Direct Messages" will ask you to verify your Florida Poly email.

Lastly, every year you will be required to attend an ethics meeting. Keep in mind:

  • Ethics meetings are mandatory to be a member of the club. Membership is required for privileges such as voting rights, being a member of our competition teams (not the weekend CTFs), and holding an officer position.
  • Once you attend an ethics meeting, you receive a full year of membership. Not just a school year!
  • You'll also need to sign a one-time ethics waiver in addition to attending the meeting. We'll have physical copies and digital copies available to sign!
